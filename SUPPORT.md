# MatchIQ Support

Thank you for using MatchIQ! We are here to help.

If you are experiencing a technical issue, have a question about the app, or would like to provide feedback, please use the contact information below. Your input is valuable and helps us improve the app for everyone.

## How to Get Help

### 1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Before reaching out, we recommend checking our FAQ section (if available in-app or on our website) for answers to common questions.

### 2. Reporting an Issue

If you encounter a bug or a technical problem, please provide as much detail as possible, including:

*   A clear description of the issue.
*   The device model and operating system version you are using.
*   Steps to reproduce the problem.
*   Screenshots or screen recordings, if applicable.

This information will help us diagnose and resolve the issue more quickly.

### 3. Contact Us

For all support inquiries, bug reports, or feedback, please email us at:

**matchiqat@gmail.com**

## Our Commitment

We are a small team dedicated to providing the best possible experience. We review every message and will do our best to respond to your inquiry as quickly as possible.

Thank you for your patience and for being a part of the MatchIQ community!
