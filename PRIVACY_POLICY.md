# Privacy Policy for MatchIQ

**Last Updated:** October 26, 2023

## 1. Introduction

MatchIQ (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is a mobile quiz application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use MatchIQ. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This policy is designed to comply with Google Play’s “Data Safety” disclosures.

## 2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve the App, we collect the following types of information.

### 2.1. Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account, we collect personal information required for your profile:

*   **Account Information (via Firebase Authentication):** When you sign up using a third-party provider like Google Sign-In, we collect your **Name**, **Email Address**, and a **Unique User ID**. This information is necessary to create, manage, and secure your account.

### 2.2. Information Collected Automatically

As you use the App, we automatically collect data related to your in-game activity and device. This data is essential for core app functionality, analytics, and stability.

*   **In-Game Data:** We store your progress and activity, including:
    *   Coins, XP, level, and achievements.
    *   Game progress, match results, and win/loss records.
    *   Leaderboard and ranking information.
    *   Quiz attempts and performance statistics.
    *   This data is stored using **Firebase Firestore** and/or **Firebase Realtime Database**.

*   **Automatically Collected Technical Data (via Firebase Services):**
    *   **Device Information:** Device type, model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
    *   **Crash and Performance Data:** We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect crash logs and diagnostic information to identify and fix bugs. This includes a unique installation UUID to diagnose issues.
    *   **Analytics and Usage Data:** We use **Firebase Analytics** to understand how you interact with the App. This includes in-app events, session duration, and feature usage patterns. This data is used in an aggregated, anonymous form to improve the user experience.
    *   **Configuration Data:** We may use **Firebase Remote Config** to update the app’s behavior and appearance without requiring an app update.

### 2.3. Advertising Identifier

If we introduce advertisements in the future, the App may collect your device’s Advertising Identifier (e.g., IDFA for iOS, GAID for Android). This identifier is used for ad personalization and attribution. We will update this policy and request your consent before collecting it.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

*   **To Provide and Maintain the Service:** To create and manage your account, save your game progress, and operate the core features of the App.
*   **To Personalize Your Experience:** To display your username, rank, and achievements within the App and on leaderboards.
*   **To Improve the App:** To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve app stability, performance, and features.
*   **To Secure Your Account:** To authenticate your access and prevent unauthorized use.

## 4. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your data is shared only with the following trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the App:

*   **Google Firebase:** We use multiple Firebase services to provide the backend infrastructure for the App. These services include:
    *   **Firebase Authentication:** For user sign-up and login.
    *   **Firebase Firestore & Realtime Database:** To store user data and game progress.
    *   **Firebase Crashlytics:** For crash reporting and stability analysis.
    *   **Firebase Analytics:** For usage analytics.
    *   **Firebase Remote Config:** For dynamic app configuration.
    *   **Firebase Cloud Storage:** (If applicable) To store user-generated content like profile pictures.

    Google’s use of this data is governed by [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services.

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. To do so, please contact us at **matchiqat@gmail.com**. Upon receiving your request, we will delete your personal data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are required by law to retain it.

## 6. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. All information is stored securely within Firebase services, which employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect data in transit and at rest. While we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, no security system is impenetrable.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information from our servers.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by displaying the updated version within the App or through other communication channels. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
