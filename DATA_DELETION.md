# MatchIQ Account and Data Deletion

Our application, MatchIQ, uses Google Sign-In for authentication. We do not store your password. The data we collect is limited to the information provided by Google, such as your name and email address, which is used for your in-app profile and leaderboards.

### How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data from MatchIQ, please send an email to:

**matchiqat@gmail.com.com**

Please use the following details in your email:
- **Subject:** Account Deletion Request
- **Body of email:** Please clearly state that you wish to delete your account and all associated data for the MatchIQ app.

Upon receiving your request, we will process it and permanently delete all of your personal data from our records within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
